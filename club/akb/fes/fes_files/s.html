We hope you enjoyed using our Founder Equity Solutions aka "FES" model. We want to make it even more precise and useful for you – and you can help us by completing this short survey. It shouldn’t take more than 10 minutes of your time.

“Equity advantage”, a term used abundantly in the questionnaire, means an additional amount of equity a founder can have. For example, an equity advantage of 10% means that a founder can have 30% of equity vs. 20% without the equity advantage.

If you already decided on how equity should be split between the founders in your startup, describe your real situation. If not, then put in the numbers which you feel would be fair for your team and your startup.
